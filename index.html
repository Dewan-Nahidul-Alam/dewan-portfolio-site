About

The analyst behind

I'm Dewan Nahidul Alam, a data and business analyst currently pursuing a Master of Business Analytics at the University of Auckland. My background spans five years across two fast-moving industries, telecom and e-commerce, where I've worked at the intersection of data, product strategy, and customer behaviour.

At Robi Axiata, one of Bangladesh's largest telecom operators, I progressed from managing a prepaid product portfolio responsible for BDT 25 million in daily revenue, to building dashboards and analytical models that shaped strategic decisions at the campaign and business level.

At Daraz Bangladesh, South Asia's leading e-commerce platform, I analyzed traffic and sales data for a platform with 9 million daily active users, ensuring product placement, campaign execution, and content quality drove measurable commercial outcomes.

I work with SQL, Python, R, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel. My goal is always the same: make data comprehensible and useful for people who need to make decisions.
